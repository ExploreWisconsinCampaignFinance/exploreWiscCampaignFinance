About

This website is an effort to shed light on who and how money is being raised to support state legislative elections. It aims to illustrate and engage citizens of Wisconsin, to show how their legislators are raising money and from whom this money is coming from. It is not meant to shame any one legislator but merely expose the system we have set up for ourselves.

Is this how we want to run our elections?
